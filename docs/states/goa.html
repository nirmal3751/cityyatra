Goa

Beaches, Parties & Portuguese Heritage

“India’s sunshine state with endless vibes.”

Goa is India’s smallest state but most vibrant. Known for its beaches, forts, churches, and nightlife. A blend of Indian and Portuguese culture makes it unique.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Panaji
  • Languages: Konkani, English
  • Famous Food: Goan Fish Curry, Bebinca
  • Main Festival: Carnival, Shigmo

Uttar Pradesh

Heartland of India

“Where Ganga flows and history lives in every corner.”

Uttar Pradesh is the land of the Taj Mahal, holy Ganga, and vibrant culture. It has great spiritual, historical, and cultural significance.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Lucknow
  • Languages: Hindi, Urdu
  • Famous Food: Tunday Kababi, Peda
  • Main Festival: Diwali, Holi

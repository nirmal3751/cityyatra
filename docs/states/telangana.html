Telangana

Land of Pearls

“Where Charminar rises and biryani defines culture.”

Telangana is known for its historical monuments, IT hub in Hyderabad, and Hyderabadi cuisine. It is one of the youngest states of India.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Hyderabad
  • Languages: Telugu, Urdu
  • Famous Food: Hyderabadi Biryani
  • Main Festival: Bathukamma, Bonalu

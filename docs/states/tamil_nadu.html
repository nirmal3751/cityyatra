Tamil Nadu

Land of Temples

“Where Dravidian architecture stands tall and traditions live on.”

Tamil Nadu is famous for its temples, Bharatanatyam, and cultural richness. It also has scenic hill stations and beaches.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Chennai
  • Languages: Tamil
  • Famous Food: Dosa, Idli, Sambar
  • Main Festival: Pongal

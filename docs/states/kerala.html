Kerala

God’s Own Country

“Where backwaters flow and nature heals every soul.”

Kerala is famous for its backwaters, Ayurveda, Kathakali dance, and serene beaches. It is also known as "God’s Own Country" due to its natural beauty and cultural richness.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Thiruvananthapuram
  • Languages: Malayalam, English
  • Famous Food: Appam with Stew, Kerala Sadya
  • Main Festival: Onam

Bihar is known for its ancient history, rich culture, and spiritual heritage. It is home to world-famous sites like Nalanda and Vikramshila universities, Bodh Gaya (where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment), and Valshali, the birthplace of Lord Mahavira. The state beautifully blends tradition with devotion, offering soulful experiences through its temples, monasteries, and festivals like Chhath Puja.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Patna
  • Languages: Hindi, Magahi, Maithili, Bhajpuri
  • Famous Food: Litti Chokha, Khaja
  • Main Festival: Chhath Puja

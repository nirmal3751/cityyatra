Haryana

Land of Milk, Sports & Heritage

“Where Mahabharata echoes and champions are born.”

Haryana is known for Kurukshetra (land of Mahabharata), agriculture, and sports personalities. Gurgaon is a major IT & corporate hub.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Chandigarh (shared with Punjab)
  • Languages: Hindi, Haryanvi
  • Famous Food: Bajra Khichdi, Churma
  • Main Festival: Teej, Holi

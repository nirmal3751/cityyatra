Assam is famous for its tea plantations, one-horned rhinoceros at Kaziranga National Park, and the mighty Brahmaputra River. It is also known for Bihu festival, silk weaving, and scenic hills.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Dispur (Guwahati)
  • Languages: Assamese, Bodo, Bengali
  • Famous Food: Fish Curry, Pitha
  • Main Festival: Bihu

Karnataka

Land of Coffee, Culture & Heritage

“From royal Mysuru to modern Bengaluru, Karnataka is a blend of past and future.”

Karnataka is famous for its IT hub Bengaluru, royal palaces in Mysuru, and Hampi’s UNESCO heritage ruins. The state is rich in coffee plantations, waterfalls, and cultural festivals like Dasara.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Bengaluru
  • Languages: Kannada, English
  • Famous Food: Bisi Bele Bath, Mysore Pak
  • Main Festival: Mysuru Dasara

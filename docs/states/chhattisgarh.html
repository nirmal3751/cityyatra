Chhattisgarh

Land of Waterfalls & Tribal Culture

“Where nature sings through forests and rivers.”

Chhattisgarh is known for its dense forests, Chitrakote Waterfalls (Niagara of India), tribal culture, and ancient temples. Bastar region is famous for art, craft, and festivals.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Raipur
  • Languages: Hindi, Chhattisgarhi
  • Famous Food: Chana Samosa, Dubki Kadhi
  • Main Festival: Bastar Dussehra

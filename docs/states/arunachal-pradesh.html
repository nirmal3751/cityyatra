Arunachal Pradesh

Land of the Rising Sun

“Where the sun first kisses India.”

Arunachal Pradesh, located in the northeast, is known for its breathtaking mountains, Buddhist monasteries, and diverse tribes. Famous spots include Tawang Monastery, Ziro Valley, and Namdapha National Park.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Itanagar
  • Languages: Hindi, English, Tribal Languages
  • Famous Food: Thukpa, Momos
  • Main Festival: Losar, Solung

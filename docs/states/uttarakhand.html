Uttarakhand

Devbhumi – Land of Gods

“Where the Himalayas meet spirituality.”

Uttarakhand is famous for its pilgrimage sites, hill stations, and natural beauty. It is also called Devbhumi for its temples and holy shrines.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Dehradun (Winter), Gairsain (Summer)
  • Languages: Hindi, Garhwali, Kumaoni
  • Famous Food: Kafuli, Bhang ki Chutney
  • Main Festival: Kumbh Mela

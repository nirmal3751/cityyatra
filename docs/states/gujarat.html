Gujarat

Land of Lions, Gir & White Desert

“Vibrant Gujarat, where culture meets progress.”

Gujarat is known for Gir National Park (Asiatic Lions), Somnath Temple, Dwarka, and Rann of Kutch. It is also the birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Gandhinagar
  • Languages: Gujarati, Hindi
  • Famous Food: Dhokla, Thepla
  • Main Festival: Navratri, Uttarayan

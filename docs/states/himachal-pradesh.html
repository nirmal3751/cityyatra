Himachal Pradesh

Land of Snowy Mountains & Valleys

“Where every mountain tells a story.”

Himachal Pradesh is known for hill stations like Shimla, Manali, Dharamshala, and Dalhousie. It is also famous for adventure sports, apple orchards, and Tibetan culture.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Shimla (Summer), Dharamshala (Winter)
  • Languages: Hindi, Pahari
  • Famous Food: Siddu, Madra
  • Main Festival: Dussehra (Kullu), Losar

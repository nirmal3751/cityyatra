Maharashtra

Land of Bollywood & Forts

“From Mumbai’s glamour to Ajanta’s caves, Maharashtra blends tradition and modernity.”

Maharashtra is India’s financial hub and home to Bollywood. Famous for Ajanta-Ellora caves, beaches, forts of Shivaji Maharaj, and vibrant festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Mumbai
  • Languages: Marathi, Hindi
  • Famous Food: Vada Pav, Puran Poli
  • Main Festival: Ganesh Chaturthi

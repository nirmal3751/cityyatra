Jharkhand

Land of Forests, Waterfalls & Minerals

“Nature’s treasure chest in eastern India.”

Jharkhand is rich in minerals, forests, and scenic waterfalls like Hundru and Dassam. Ranchi, Jamshedpur, and Dhanbad are major cities.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Ranchi
  • Languages: Hindi, Santhali, Nagpuri
  • Famous Food: Dhuska, Rugra Curry
  • Main Festival: Sarhul, Karma

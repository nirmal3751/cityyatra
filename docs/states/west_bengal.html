West Bengal

Land of Culture & Literature

“Where art, literature, and festivals thrive together.”

West Bengal is famous for Durga Puja, literature, sweets, and colonial heritage. It has Himalayas in the north and the Bay of Bengal in the south.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Kolkata
  • Languages: Bengali, Hindi
  • Famous Food: Rasgulla, Fish Curry
  • Main Festival: Durga Puja

Punjab

Land of Five Rivers

“Where the Golden Temple shines and Bhangra beats echo.”

Punjab is famous for its fertile land, Punjabi culture, Golden Temple, and lively spirit. It is also the land of bhangra and delicious food.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Chandigarh
  • Languages: Punjabi, Hindi
  • Famous Food: Makki di Roti & Sarson da Saag
  • Main Festival: Baisakhi, Lohri

Andhra Pradesh

Land of Temples, Beaches & Culture

“Where devotion meets the sea breeze and heritage whispers through ancient stones.”

Andhra Pradesh is famous for its rich culture, temples, and beautiful coastline. From the Tirupati Balaji temple to the beaches of Visakhapatnam, the state offers a blend of spirituality and natural beauty. It is also known for Kuchipudi dance and spicy Andhra cuisine.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Amaravati (Proposed), Visakhapatnam
  • Languages: Telugu, Urdu
  • Famous Food: Pulihora, Gongura Pachadi
  • Main Festival: Ugadi

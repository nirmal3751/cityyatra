Andhra Pradesh

Land of Temples, Coastlines & Culture

Andhra Pradesh is known for its rich cultural heritage, historic temples, and scenic coastline along the Bay of Bengal. It is home to classical dance forms like Kuchipudi and vibrant festivals like Ugadi and Sankranti. The state blends tradition with modernity, offering soulful experiences from hill stations to heritage towns.

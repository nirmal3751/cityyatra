Madhya Pradesh is called the "Heart of India". It is famous for Khajuraho temples, Sanchi Stupa, Gwalior Fort, and wildlife sanctuaries like Kanha and Bandhavgarh.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Bhopal
  • Languages: Hindi
  • Famous Food: Poha, Bhutte ka Kees
  • Main Festival: Khajuraho Dance Festival

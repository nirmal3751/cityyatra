Odisha

Land of Temples & Tribes

“Where the Rath Yatra echoes and waves meet ancient temples.”

Odisha is known for the Jagannath Temple of Puri, Konark Sun Temple, Chilika Lake, and tribal culture. It has a rich history of art and architecture.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Bhubaneswar
  • Languages: Odia
  • Famous Food: Dalma, Pakhala Bhata
  • Main Festival: Rath Yatra

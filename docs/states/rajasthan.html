Rajasthan

Land of Kings

“Where deserts sing, forts stand tall, and palaces shine.”

Rajasthan is famous for its forts, palaces, deserts, and vibrant culture. Jaipur, Jodhpur, and Udaipur are world-famous tourist attractions.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Jaipur
  • Languages: Hindi, Rajasthani
  • Famous Food: Dal Baati Churma, Gatte ki Sabzi
  • Main Festival: Desert Festival, Teej

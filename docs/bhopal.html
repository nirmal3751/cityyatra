City Yatra – Bhopal

Bhopal, the capital of Madhya Pradesh, is a city of contrasts — serene lakes and Mughal architecture meet tribal heritage and buzzing street food. Whether you're strolling around Upper Lake or sipping Suleimani chai in the old city, Bhopal offers a soulful experience that stays with you.

About Bhopal

  • Founded by: Raja Bhoj (11th century)
  • Population: ~1.8 million
  • Elevation: 528 m
  • Official Language: Hindi
  • Nickname: City of Lakes
  • Famous for: Lakes, heritage, street food, and historical landmarks

Top Attractions

  • Upper Lake: Boating and sunset views
  • Van Vihar: Nature park with free-roaming animals
  • Bhimbetka: Ancient cave paintings
  • Manav Museum: Museum of Humankind
  • Gohar Mahal: Nawabi-era palace

Local Food Highlights

  • Suleimani Chai
  • Poha-Jalebi
  • Biryani & Kebabs
  • Bhopali Gosht Korma

Travel Tips

  • Best Time: October to March
  • Transport: Rail, road, air + autos
  • Nearby: Sanchi, Pachmarhi, Ujjain

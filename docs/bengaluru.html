City Yatra – Bengaluru

Bengaluru, India’s tech capital, blends innovation with tradition. From Cubbon Park to startup hubs, it’s a city where ideas bloom and filter coffee fuels dreams.

About Bengaluru

  • Founded by: Kempe Gowda (16th century)
  • Population: ~12 million
  • Elevation: 920 m
  • Official Language: Kannada
  • Nickname: Silicon Valley of India
  • Famous for: IT parks, gardens, weather, startups

Top Attractions

  • Lalbagh Botanical Garden
  • Cubbon Park
  • Bangalore Palace
  • ISKCON Temple
  • UB City

Local Food Highlights

  • Masala Dosa
  • Filter Coffee
  • Ragi Mudde
  • Chow Chow Bath

Travel Tips

  • Best Time: October to February
  • Transport: Metro, BMTC buses, autos
  • Nearby: Nandi Hills, Mysuru, Coorg

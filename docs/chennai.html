City Yatra – Chennai

Chennai, the cultural capital of South India, blends ancient temples, colonial architecture, and a thriving tech scene. From Marina Beach sunrises to filter coffee in Mylapore, Chennai is a city that breathes heritage and modernity together.

About Chennai

  • Founded by: British East India Company (17th century)
  • Population: ~7 million
  • Elevation: 6 m
  • Official Language: Tamil
  • Nickname: Detroit of India
  • Famous for: Temples, beaches, classical music, and IT industry

Top Attractions

  • Marina Beach: Longest urban beach in India
  • Kapaleeshwarar Temple: Dravidian architecture and devotion
  • Fort St. George: Colonial-era fort and museum
  • Santhome Basilica: Historic church built over St. Thomas’s tomb
  • Mahabalipuram: UNESCO site just outside the city

Local Food Highlights

  • Filter Coffee: Strong, aromatic, and served in steel tumbler
  • Dosa & Sambar: Breakfast staple
  • Sundal: Beachside snack made of chickpeas
  • Chettinad Cuisine: Spicy and flavorful meat dishes

Travel Tips

  • Best Time: November to February
  • Transport: Metro, buses, autos, suburban trains
  • Nearby: Pondicherry, Mahabalipuram, Kanchipuram

City Yatra – Mumbai

Mumbai, the financial capital of India, is a city that never sleeps. From Marine Drive sunsets to the buzz of Bollywood, Mumbai blends dreams, chaos, and charm like no other.

About Mumbai

  • Founded by: Portuguese, later British (17th century)
  • Population: ~20 million
  • Elevation: 14 m
  • Official Language: Marathi
  • Nickname: City of Dreams
  • Famous for: Bollywood, finance, street food, colonial architecture

Top Attractions

  • Gateway of India
  • Marine Drive
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus
  • Elephanta Caves
  • Juhu Beach

Local Food Highlights

  • Vada Pav
  • Pav Bhaji
  • Bombay Sandwich
  • Misal Pav

Travel Tips

  • Best Time: November to February
  • Transport: Local trains, BEST buses, metro
  • Nearby: Lonavala, Alibaug, Matheran

About Vijayawada

Nestled on the banks of the Krishna River, Vijayawada is a vibrant city that blends spirituality, commerce, and culture. Known for the iconic Kanaka Durga Temple perched atop Indrakeeladri Hill, the city pulses with devotion during festivals like Dasara.

Must-Visit Places

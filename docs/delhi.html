City Yatra – Delhi

Delhi, the capital of India, is a city of layers — Mughal grandeur, colonial charm, and modern pulse. From India Gate to Chandni Chowk, Delhi is history and hustle in one frame.

About Delhi

  • Founded by: Multiple dynasties over centuries
  • Population: ~19 million
  • Elevation: 216 m
  • Official Language: Hindi
  • Nickname: Dilwalon ki Dilli
  • Famous for: Monuments, street food, politics, culture

Top Attractions

  • Red Fort
  • Qutub Minar
  • India Gate
  • Lotus Temple
  • Humayun’s Tomb

Local Food Highlights

  • Chole Bhature
  • Parathas from Parathe Wali Gali
  • Golgappe
  • Kebabs

Travel Tips

  • Best Time: October to March
  • Transport: Metro, buses, autos
  • Nearby: Agra, Jaipur, Haridwar
